Rules

Rules may be updated in due time if needed.
Failing to comply with the rules, will be taken into serious consideration against your participation.


General Rules:


  • All event-related queries are handled on Discord, so once registered join the channel for more information.

  • You are welcome to register as a team of five, but if you register as a solo participant, we will combine you with other solo participants to make a team of five for the match. We will form the teams in consideration that they will all be balanced at the game level.

  • The match schedule for round-1 will be made available on the discord and our website by 10 am on 04-09-2021. The match schedule for the next round will be announced 10 minutes prior to each round. No alterations to the schedule will be considered.

  • The event starts at 12:00 pm on 04-09-2021, so please be on time.

  • We will conduct all rounds in continuation on 04-09-2021 with small breaks in between, so please plan the day’s agenda accordingly.

  • The matches will be played in custom mode, and the selection of players will be done via fcfs (no excuses if you miss your favorite character).

  • The OWASPVIT will provide two spectators for each match to maintain decorum. He/She/They will be the immediate point of contact for u during the match. It is mandatory to follow the instructions of the member.

  • Technical problems, internet issues, etc. during the match are solely the responsibility of the participant, so please check all the points before the match begins.

  • A spin wheel system will be used to select the map. This will ensure absolute transparency.

  • During the match, players are allowed to use team VC and chat for some casual chats among themselves. Kindly don’t spam in the channel. Use of abusive/vulgar language is strictly prohibited.

  • The winners of each round will automatically proceed to the next round. In case if an odd number of teams qualifies for a round, then the team with the highest average combat score will proceed to the next round without playing the match again.


Basic Roleplay Rules:

  • The decision of OWASPVIT members will be final and abiding. Disrespecting us or our decision would be taken into serious consideration.
